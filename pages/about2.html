About Us

Wellness Nutrition, the pioneer Weight Management company in Chennai is the brain-child of our founders - Mr. Susikumar, Dr. sabin & Ms. Nithya. After a comprehensive study of the “Weight Loss” market in Chennai, they saw a need to bring about a change to the unstructured, unorganized, and unhealthy practitioners, in the best interest of people who were genuinely looking for help regarding their weight, but were unable to find reliable sources. Wootu Nutrition was established in 2016 to provide professional, scientific, and healthy solutions to people battling weight issues.

At Wellness, we believe what we are today is result of what lifestyle we follow . Our health reflects our lifestyle and in turn, our lifestyle often influences our health. We carefully examine each individual’s current Lifestyle habits, and their current health condition. We understand that Weight management is a long-term approach to a healthy life style. It includes a balance of healthy eating and physical exercise to equate energy expenditure and energy intake. Considering your needs and lifestyle parameters we offer customized and strategized nutrition plans to assist you in achieving your health goals.
